Welcome!

"Team-based learning" is a pedagogical strategy that consists of combining activities in a structured way to promote group study and improve the quality of learning of individuals in the group. The "Team-based learning" methodology consists of modules that are divided into a cycle of three phases: preparation, classroom tests, and application exercises focused on the topic being discussed.
